Singular and Plural

Learn the difference between singular and plural with examples!

Key Points

  • Singular means one (e.g., cat, dog).
  • Plural means more than one (e.g., cats, dogs).
  • To make a word plural, we often add *-s* or *-es* (e.g., book ‚Üí books).
  • Some words have irregular plurals (e.g., child ‚Üí children).
